Introducing the Coding Wand!

Posted by Gabriel Hochstatter

Have you ever wanted to have your own programmable magic wand? Well today might be your lucky day!

Coming in October of this year, our Kano Magic Wand Coding Kit lets you build your very own magic wand, and program it to do almost anything! With over 70 step-by-step challenges you will learn to code, and also make some magic while you're at it!

The coding wand is compatible with iOS and Android mobile devices along with Mac and PC computers, so everyone can get a taste of the magic.
